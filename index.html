Challenges

Affordable housing is necessary and numerous solutions claim to offer resilient constructions to help face some of the most devastating challenges faced by a large portion of our global population.

Flammable Materials

Fire is arguably one of the most dangerous and proliferent enemies of home owners worldwide. Inferior building materials are often the cause of destructive fires.

Hurricane Sensitivity

Storms such as hurricanes, tornados and cyclones are affecting the most vulnerable populations in the world. FlexBuild makes the difference between a blown-away home or just some window damage.

Biohazard Hospitable

Around 429,000 people die of malaria every year. 80% of malaria transmission takes place in the home. That's why improved housing is needed to keep malaria at bay.

Home Security

Current housing does not offer sufficient protection against unauthorized entry. Specifically women and children are vulnerable to disruptions in society, exposing them to violence.

Unmovable Construction

Traditional housing is almost always bound to a single location. It's hard to move somewhere else when a political or environmental situation requires people to pack their belongings.
